Core Services

At CABER Inspection Services Ltd, we deliver reliable inspection, testing, certification, and verification services across industries to ensure quality, safety, and compliance. Our expertise covers crude oil and gas inspection, bulk cargo verification, product certification, and shipping logistics. With advanced laboratories and experienced professionals, we provide accurate, transparent, and internationally compliant results that build trust and support seamless trade operations.

  • Inspection of Bulk Cargoes
  • Crude Oil and Gas Inspection
  • Certification of Manufactured and Semi-Manufactured Products
  • Shipping Logistics & Protocols
  • Custody Transfer Documentation

About Caber

At CABER Inspection Services Ltd, we are a proudly Nigerian-owned inspection company delivering trusted inspection, testing, certification, and verification services across multiple industries.

With decades of combined experience, our experts ensure that every product, shipment, and operation meets international standards of quality, safety, and compliance. Through strong partnerships with global laboratories, we blend local knowledge with world-class expertise to provide accurate and reliable results.

Supported by state-of-the-art laboratories and a multidisciplinary team of professionals, CABER offers complete technical assurance — from production quality control to trade verification and compliance audits. We remain committed to transparency, integrity, and regulatory excellence, helping clients achieve confidence and value in every transaction.

Our Process

At CABER Inspection Services Ltd, we follow a systematic process built on precision, transparency, and compliance. From assessment and sampling to laboratory testing and certification, every stage ensures accuracy and client confidence.

Step 1
Assessment & Planning

Understanding project requirements, identifying key objectives, and defining inspection parameters.

Step 2
Inspection & Sampling

Field inspections and sample collection following safety and international standards.

Step 3
Testing & Verification

Laboratory analysis and verification to ensure compliance with international benchmarks.

Step 4
Certification & Reporting

Issuing reports and certifications, providing complete transparency and client assurance.
