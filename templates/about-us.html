About Caber

We are a proudly Nigerian-owned inspection company delivering trusted inspection, testing, certification, and verification services across multiple industries.

With decades of combined experience, our experts ensure that every product, shipment, and operation meets international standards of quality, safety, and compliance. Through strong partnerships with global laboratories, we blend local knowledge with world-class expertise to provide accurate and reliable results.

Supported by state-of-the-art laboratories and a multidisciplinary team of professionals, CABER offers complete technical assurance — from production quality control to trade verification and compliance audits. We remain committed to transparency, integrity, and regulatory excellence, helping clients achieve confidence and value in every transaction.

Why Choose Us

We stand out as a trusted partner in quality assurance, inspection, and certification across industries. Our reputation is built on accuracy, integrity, and professionalism — ensuring every service we deliver meets the highest international standards. With a deep understanding of both local and global compliance frameworks, we bridge technical expertise with transparency and efficiency to protect your business interests from production to shipment.

Our team of seasoned professionals — including engineers, chemists, surveyors, and maritime experts — combine advanced analytical methods with state-of-the-art technology to deliver dependable, verifiable, and timely results. Whether it’s oil and gas inspection, product certification, or custody transfer documentation, CABER provides the confidence and clarity you need for smooth trade operations and regulatory compliance.
