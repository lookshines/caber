HSE

Health, Safety and Environment

Building a culture of continuous improvement in Health, Safety, and Environmental performance.

  • Ensure compliance with all applicable laws, regulations, and standards on health, safety, and environmental protection.
  • Identify and mitigate potential hazards through proactive safety management and risk assessment.
  • Encourage open communication and collaboration among employees, contractors, and stakeholders on HSE matters.
  • Review and update HSE policies regularly to maintain effectiveness and relevance.
  • Promote sustainability by minimizing resource consumption, waste generation, and emissions.
  • Engage local communities and stakeholders to raise HSE awareness and contribute positively to host environments.
  • CABER Inspection Services Ltd is committed to achieving excellence in HSE performance across all operations — from project planning and execution to reporting and review.

By adhering to these principles, CABER Inspection Services Ltd fosters a safe, healthy, and environmentally responsible workplace — enhancing the well-being of our people, communities, and clients.

This HSE policy reflects our unwavering commitment to excellence, integrity, and sustainable operations, guiding all employees and stakeholders toward shared accountability in achieving our HSE goals.
